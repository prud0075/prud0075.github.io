HTML Tags

Heading <h1>
Headings are ranked in importance, h1 being the highest ranked. Each number that follows decreases in importance
Abbreviation <abbr>
When you use the abbreviation tag you must add the full name of what you would like to abbreviate. The abbreviation tag will then do its job to shorten the name using the first letter of each word seperated by a space
Anchor Tag <a>
The anchor tag is used to access links
Paragraph <p>
The paragraph tag allows you to add your own desired information
Div Tag <div>
The div tag is used to devide your information into sections
